About The Ministry

Time Of Life Centre Ministries is a registered non‑profit Christian ministry (NPO: 2021/988503/08) based in Vlakbult / Mdladla in Nkomazi, Mpumalanga, South Africa.

The ministry is led by Rafael Michael and carries a divine mandate to preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ with truth, power, healing, deliverance and prophetic ministry to the nations.

Our platforms exist to declare the Word of God, demonstrate the fire of the Holy Spirit, and edify the body of Christ.
