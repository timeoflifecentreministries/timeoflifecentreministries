About The Ministry

Time Of Life Centre Ministries is a registered non-profit Christian ministry (NPO: 2021/988503/08) based in Vlakbult / Mdladla in Nkomazi, Mpumalanga, South Africa.

The ministry is led by Rafael Michael and carries a divine mandate to preach the Gospel with truth, power, healing, deliverance and prophetic ministry.

Through the preaching of the Word of God, prayer and the power of the Holy Spirit, the ministry seeks to bring salvation, restoration, healing and hope to individuals, families and communities.
