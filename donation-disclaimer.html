Tithes, Offerings & Giving Disclaimer

Time Of Life Centre Ministries

1. Voluntary Giving

All tithes, offerings, donations, and partnership contributions to Time Of Life Centre Ministries are voluntary and given freely by individuals who wish to support the work of the ministry.

2. Use of Donations

Funds received may be used for ministry activities including church services, outreach programs, evangelism, community support, and operational needs of the ministry.

3. No Refund Policy

Donations and offerings given to the ministry are generally non-refundable unless required by applicable law.

4. Transparency

Time Of Life Centre Ministries strives to steward donations responsibly for the advancement of the Gospel and ministry work.

5. Contact

If you have questions regarding giving or donations, please contact the ministry.
